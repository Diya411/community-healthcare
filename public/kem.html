KING EDWARD MEMORIAL HOSPITAL

King Edward Memorial Hospital and Seth Gordhandas Sunderdas Medical College is one of the foremost medical colleges and hospital in India, located in Mumbai, Maharashtra. It was founded in 1926; it is affiliated with Maharashtra University of Health Sciences (MUHS)

About K.E.M.

  • Capacity: 1800+ bedded multi-specialized
  • Hospital annually attends to approximately 1.8 million out-patients and 85,000 in-patients.
  • It offers comprehensive medical and surgical care, providing basic services as well as advanced treatments across all specialties.
  • Funded by the Municipal Corporation of Greater, Mumbai.
  • Most of the services are free of cost.
