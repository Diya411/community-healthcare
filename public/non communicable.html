Non-Communicable Diseases Programs

These programs aim to combat non-communicable diseases through prevention, awareness, and accessible treatment for the well-being of all citizens.

National Tobacco Control Programme (NTCP)

Addresses tobacco addiction through awareness campaigns, cessation support, and regulation enforcement.

National Programme for Prevention and Control of Cancer, Diabetes, Cardiovascular Diseases & Stroke (NPCDCS)

Promotes prevention and management of major non-communicable diseases.

National Oral Health programme

The National Oral Health Programme aims to improve oral health awareness, prevention, and treatment across communities to reduce oral diseases.

National Mental Health Programme

Provides mental health support, treatment, and awareness to promote mental well-being.
