Ayushman Bharat Yojna

Ayushmann Bharat Digital Mission - Building Digital Health Ecosystem

Ayushman Bharat Yojana is also known as PM-Jay, is a health insurance scheme by the Indian government. It provides free medical coverage up to Rs.5,00,000 per year for poor families covering treatment for serious illness and hospital stays. The scheme is Cashless meaning patient do not have to pay anything at the time of treatment. It covers over 50 cror people and can be used at both government and private hospitals across India. The process is paperless and easy to access.

Key Features:

  1. PM Jai is the world's largest health insurance scheme fully financed by government
  2. It provide a cover of 5,00,000 per family per year across public and private impanelled hospitals in India
  3. Over 12 crore poor and vulnerable entitled families are eligible for these benefits
  4. PM-JAY provides cashless access to healthcare services for beneficiary at point of service that is the hospital
  5. It covers up to 3 days of Pre-hospital and 15 days Post-hospitalisation expenses.
  6. There is no restriction on the family size, age or gender
  7. Services include approximately 1929 procedures covering all costs.
  8. Benefit of the scheme are portable across the country


Diseases covered under Aushman Bharat Yojana

  • Cancer
  • cardiovascular diseases
  • Parkinson's disease
  • kidney failure
  • burns
  • neonantal diseases
  • Mental diseases


Process for ABHA(Ayushmann Bharat Health account) card

  1. Eligibility Check:
    • All Indian citizens are eligible to create an ABHA card.
    • There is no need to be a beneficiary of the Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY) to get the ABHA card.
  2. Registration: You can register for an ABHA card in the following ways:
    • Through the Official ABDM Portal
      • Visit the ABDM Portal: https://healthid.ndhm.gov.in/
      • Click on “Create ABHA” or “Register”
      • Enter your mobile number, Aadhaar number (optional), and other personal details. You will be asked for your consent to link your Aadhaar (if you're using it for identification).
      • Provide a valid mobile number for OTP-based verification.
      • Complete the registration process by verifying the OTP sent to your mobile phone.
    • Through the ABHA Mobile App
      • Download the official ABHA App from the Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
      • Open the app and follow the same registration process as above, with mobile number and OTP verification.
  3. Linking Health Records:
    • Once you have created your ABHA ID, you can link your health records from any healthcare provider who is registered under the Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission.
    • This will allow healthcare providers to access your health history and offer seamless care.
  4. Card Generation:
    • After successful registration, your ABHA Card will be generated.
    • You will be able to download the ABHA Card digitally in the form of a QR code or a PDF.
    • You can print the card if you wish, or just use it in its digital form for accessing health services.
  5. Using the ABHA Card:
    • You can use the ABHA card at registered healthcare providers, hospitals, or diagnostic centers to avail of services.
    • The health providers will access your health data through the unique ABHA ID.
Important Notes:
  • While registration is free, linking your Aadhaar number to your ABHA card is optional but recommended to avoid duplicate entries.
  • The system is integrated with multiple health schemes, but your eligibility for government health schemes depends on your specific scheme enrollment.
