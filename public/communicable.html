Communicable Diseases Programs

These programs aim to prevent and control communicable diseases through awareness, accessible treatment, and effective interventions to protect public health.

Integrated Disease Surveillance Programme (IDSP)

Focuses on monitoring and responding to outbreaks of communicable diseases across India.

Revised National Tuberculosis Control Programme (RNTCP)

Promotes prevention and management of major non-communicable diseases.

National Oral Health programme

Dedicated to controlling and eventually eradicating tuberculosis through early diagnosis and treatment.

National Leprosy Eradication Programme (NLEP)

Aims to completely eradicate leprosy through effective early detection and treatment.
