HEALTHY AND BALANCED DIET

NEED OF HEALTHY AND BALANCED DIET

A healthy and balanced diet is crucial for providing the body with essential nutrients like vitamins, minerals, and macronutrients needed for energy, growth, and repair. It helps prevent chronic diseases, supports mental and physical well-being, and maintains a healthy weight. Additionally, it boosts immunity, promotes healthy aging, and supports proper development in children and adolescents, ensuring overall vitality and longevity.

CARBOHYDRATES

Carbohydrates are the primary energy source for the body. They fuel brain function, physical activity, and support the metabolism of proteins and fats. Simple carbs provide quick energy, while complex carbs offer sustained energy release, helping to maintain stable blood sugar levels.

Need: 225-325 grams (45-65% of daily calories)

PROTEINS

Proteins are essential for building and repairing tissues, including muscles, skin, and organs. They play a crucial role in producing enzymes and hormones, supporting immune function, and maintaining muscle mass, especially during physical activity or recovery from injury.

Need: 50-175 grams (10-35% of daily calories)

FATS

Fats are vital for long-term energy storage, insulation, and protecting vital organs. They are key components of cell membranes and are necessary for absorbing fat-soluble vitamins (A, D, E, and K). Healthy fats, like omega-3s, also reduce inflammation and support brain health.

Need: 44-77 grams (20-35% of daily calories)

Minerals

Minerals are essential for numerous bodily functions: they support bone health (calcium), enable oxygen transport in the blood (iron), and help maintain fluid balance (potassium). They are also involved in nerve function, muscle contraction, and enzyme activity (zinc). Adequate mineral intake is crucial for overall health, preventing deficiencies that can lead to various health issues.

Need: Varies by mineral, e.g., Calcium: 1,000-1,300 mg, Iron: 8-18 mg
