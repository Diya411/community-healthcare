Family-Friendly Indian Diet Plan

Breakfast: 20–25% of daily calories → 300-400 kcal

  • Idli with sambhar and coconut chutney + a glass of fresh orange juice (Approx. 350-400 kcal)
  • Poha with peanuts and coriander + a glass of buttermilk (Approx. 300 kcal)
  • Oats porridge with chopped fruits and nuts + green tea (Approx. 250 kcal)
  • Boiled eggs with whole wheat toast and a glass of milk (Approx. 300-350 kcal)
  • Scrambled eggs with multigrain bread + a glass of fresh fruit juice (Approx. 350 kcal)
  • Omelette with vegetables, whole wheat bread + herbal tea (Approx. 300 kcal)

Lunch: 30–35% of daily calories → 400-600 kcal

  • Mixed vegetable curry, dal tadka, steamed rice, and cucumber salad (Approx. 400-450 kcal)
  • Rajma chawal (kidney beans with rice) and a side of carrot-beetroot salad (Approx. 500 kcal)
  • Baingan bharta with bajra roti and onion-tomato salad (Approx. 400 kcal)
  • Chicken curry with steamed rice and green salad (Approx. 500-550 kcal)
  • Mutton biryani with cucumber raita and onion rings (Approx. 600 kcal)
  • Prawn curry with rice and a side of green beans stir-fry (Approx. 500 kcal)

Snacks: 10–15% of daily calories → 100-275 kcal

  • Roasted chana or a handful of nuts with masala tea (Approx. 200-250 kcal)
  • A bowl of fresh fruits or a fruit smoothie (Approx. 150-200 kcal)
  • Corn chaat with lemon juice and spices (Approx. 150-200 kcal)
  • Grilled chicken skewers or boiled eggs (Approx. 250-300 kcal)
  • A bowl of boiled chickpeas or sprouts with lemon and spices (Approx. 200-250 kcal)
  • Roasted peanuts or boiled eggs with masala (Approx. 200 kcal)

Dinner: 30–35% of daily calories → 400-600 kcal

  • Palak paneer with roti and mixed vegetable raita (Approx. 400-450 kcal)
  • Stuffed paratha (paneer or potato) with mint chutney and curd (Approx. 350-400 kcal)
  • Matar paneer with jeera rice and a bowl of curd (Approx. 450 kcal)
  • Fish curry with roti and sautéed vegetables (Approx. 400-450 kcal)
  • Tandoori chicken with naan and a bowl of curd (Approx. 500 kcal)
  • Egg curry with paratha and green salad (Approx. 400-450 kcal)
