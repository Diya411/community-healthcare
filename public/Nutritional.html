National Nutritional Programmes

Government initiatives for improved nutritional health across India.

National Iodine Deficiency Disorders Control Programme (NIDDCP)

This program aims to eliminate iodine deficiency disorders (IDD) through regular monitoring and fortification of edible salt with iodine.

MAA (Mothers’ Absolute Affection) Programme

The MAA Programme promotes breastfeeding practices to ensure better health for infants and young children through improved breastfeeding support.

National Programme for Prevention and Control of Fluorosis (NPPCF)

NPPCF aims to prevent and control fluorosis caused by excessive fluoride exposure, especially from drinking water, by ensuring safe water sources.

National Iron Plus Initiative for Anaemia Control

This initiative provides iron supplementation to combat anemia in children, adolescents, and pregnant women across India.
